About

Borrowed Time Fine Art Gallery is McKinney's only upscale tattoo gallery maintaining the highest in industry standards along with the best customer service.

In line with our promise to deliver the best customer service comes our brand new online quoting and booking system where you'll be able to submit reference photos, receive a quote from our talented artists, and put a deposit down all from the comfort of your own home!

So what are you waiting for? Feel free to submit that quote form and we'll be sure to reach out to you to let you know how long to schedule your appointment for and get on our books today! You can even see the availability of your favorite artists!
