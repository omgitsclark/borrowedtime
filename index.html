ABOUT US:

Graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and currently holding a job as a software developer. Growing up I was either playing games, helping fix the family cars, or shooting things in the back yard. Pretty much, if i saw a video of something cool, I made it my mission to try it out. This inevitably sparked the stupid amount of hobbies that I struggle to keep up with to this day.
On a more personal note, I married my highschool sweetheart (who I met outside a Fry's Electronics store on Black Friday) in 2018. We have three dogs (Benny, Barry, and Betty) and are traveling as much as we can. Every day I'm thankful for the amount of crazy hobbies (and the messes those hobbies spawn) she puts up with 24/7.
