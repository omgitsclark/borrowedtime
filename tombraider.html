About Tomb Raider

With over 4 years of experience, Tomb Raider has become well known for her floral tattoos. This is due to her impeccable attention to detail, dead steady hand, and perfect technique. Combining crisp lines and masterful shading, Tomb Raider is sure to provide designs that will make you feel like you can almost reach out and grab or even feel on the skin.

Specializes In

  • Floral Designs - Intricate and lifelike botanical artwork
  • Fine Line Work - Crisp, clean linework with precision
  • Shading Mastery - Expert dimensional shading techniques
