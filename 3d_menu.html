3D Printing

My Current Setup

Left to right: Ender3 Pro, CR-10s, Ender3 Modified

My first interaction with 3D printing was back in my Senior Year of college in my senior design class. We were making a gimbal using the cheapest parts that we could to compete with the market. This included 3D printing the brackets to hold the camera, motors, and sensors in place.

Fast forward to post grad life, I had started to accumulate so many things that I needed a way to get everything organized. That's when it hit me. Buy a 3D printer to make containers so that you can organize everything. A few hundred dollars later, I dived straight into the hobby that is 3D printing (and haven't managed to make anything useful to organize my stuff).
